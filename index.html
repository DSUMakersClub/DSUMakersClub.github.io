Link to our main repo.

The DSU Makers Club is fully ran by students and operates as part of the Douglas Students' Union Clubs.


The goal of the club is to gather students who have a passion for DIY projects and pool our skills to realize projects that would not be doable by our selves.

We have members that Knit, Sew, leatherwork, woodwork, 3D model, craft props, and everything in between!
